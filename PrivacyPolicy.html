Privacy Policy

Home Expenses Mobile
Last updated: June 2, 2026

Overview

Home Expenses Mobile is a personal, private mobile web application designed to help you easily enter expense entries into your personal Google Sheets spreadsheet. This application runs entirely within your own Google Account environment using Google Apps Script. We do not host, store, or transmit your private information to any external servers.

Data We Access & Request

To provide its core functionalities, the application requests access to the following Google User Data scopes:

  • Google Sheets (Spreadsheet access): To read category structures, read connected spreadsheet names, and append expense rows (One-Time and Recurrent mode) directly to your active sheet.
  • Google Drive: To locate and automatically connect to your Home Expenses YYYY spreadsheets (specifically for the optional Jan 1st Auto Year-Switch feature).
  • External Requests (Gemini API): If (and only if) you explicitly enable "AI Insights" in the app's settings and provide your own Gemini API Key, the application makes external API calls to Google's Gemini models to generate financial analysis. No other external connections or tracking scripts are loaded.

How Your Data is Handled and Stored

  • Zero Server-Side Hosting: There is no central server, database, or third-party storage. All operational code runs either on Google's own Apps Script servers under your credentials, or locally within your web browser.
  • Local Storage & Script Properties: The ID of your chosen spreadsheet and configuration preferences (like toggling AI insights or Year-Switch) are stored in Google's secure Apps Script User/Script Properties and your browser's local/session storage.
  • Your Spreadsheets: Your financial data never leaves your Google account unless you configure AI analytics, in which case selected monthly aggregate numbers are sent securely via HTTPS directly to Google's Gemini API endpoint.

Google API Limited Use Disclosure:
Home Expenses Mobile's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

User Controls and Data Deletion

Since all data is stored inside your own Google Drive and Google Sheets, you are in complete control of your data:

  • You can inspect, modify, or delete your expense entries at any time directly inside your Google Sheets.
  • You can disconnect or delete the application from your Google Account entirely by visiting your Google Account's Third-Party Access settings page and revoking permissions.
